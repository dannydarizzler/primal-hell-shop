Create your account

Sign up with your Discord User ID and a password. This links your Shop account to your Discord identity.

2

Top up Primal Coins

Pay with PayPal in the Shop tab. Primal Coins are added to your account balance instantly after payment.

3

Spend your Coins

Use your Primal Coins to buy item packs directly in the Shop tab, or open Mystery Chests for a random reward.

4

Claim your items

After your purchase, open a ticket on Discord so a staff member can hand it to you in-game.

Where do my items go? Every purchase appears in your My Items tab as "Active" until a staff member marks it as delivered. Open a Discord ticket to claim it — don't wait if it's been a while.
